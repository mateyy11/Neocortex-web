Development

"How we do it"

With a team of experienced developers and a focus on delivering innovative solutions, we are equipped to handle projects of any size and complexity.

Technologies used

Neocortex uses advanced tech to provide web dev, data analytics, and cyber security solutions tailored to clients' needs. Our developers are proficient in Ruby, Python, and SQL and use frameworks like Ruby on Rails, AngularJS, and Vue.js. We also use data visualization, machine learning, and security testing tools like OWASP, Python Security, and SELinux for high-quality, secure solutions.

Dev process

Our development process at Neocortex starts with understanding client needs, followed by planning and design, building and testing, and deployment with maintenance. We prioritize open communication and quality in every step to ensure successful delivery on time and within budget.
